> Every webpage it's divided by two parts.

HEAD

> Which it's conformed by the *TITLE* of the wpage and the *URL* which contains foundamental settings for the entire wpage.

HTML

> This is the content of the entire page such as body, pictures, vids, hyperlinks and other stuff.

For that reason of hierarchy, it's highly recommended to follow the *html labels* and structure
